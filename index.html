What is
Stickletics?

"A sequence of stretches, activations, and total body exercises using a simple training stick."

Developed by sports scientist Ramin Waraghai, Stickletics combines stretching, strength, and stability work into a unique, science-based fitness experience.

Why Use a Stick?

Supports your balance

Adds stability during challenging poses and movements.

Activates your shoulders and core

Engages key muscle groups by pressing or pulling the stick.

Helps you stretch deeper

Provides leverage to safely increase your range of motion.

Guides proper form and movement control

Encourages alignment and body awareness throughout each exercise.
